DR. AKHILESH DAS GUPTA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

AND MANAGEMENT

Dr. Akhilesh Das Gupta Institute of Technology and Management was established in 2003. The institute offers following Undergraduate and Postgraduate level full-time Professional programs such as B.Tech, BBA, MBA. Admission to these courses is done through entrance exams such as IPU-CET, JEE-MAINS, CAT/ MAT/ XAT. The application mode for admission is online.
